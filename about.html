About Yongrun Printing

Founded in 2003, we have over 20 years of professional printing experience. We are a comprehensive service provider integrating development, prototyping, design, printing, processing, and packaging.

Specialized in children's enlightenment, intelligence development, games, and custom card sets. Our representative products include holographic cards, learning flashcards, playing cards, board game cards, tarot cards, and more.

With multiple advanced card production lines, our annual production capacity exceeds millions of sets. We are committed to providing high-quality, personalized one-stop printing solutions for our global clients.
